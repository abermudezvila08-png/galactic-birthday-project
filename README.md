# Galactic Birthday Project 🌍🎂🚀

## A Cinematic Science Storytelling Project

Earth is approaching its 21st Galactic Birthday.

This project transforms Earth's 4.54-billion-year history into the life story of a young woman about to celebrate her 21st birthday.

### Mission

Create a scientifically accurate and emotionally powerful film capable of inspiring young people to discover science, astronomy, geology, biology and humanity's place in the Universe.
